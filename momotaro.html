Momotaro (Japanese: 桃太郎）is a well-known Japanese fairy tell. The story is about a child born from a peach who was raised by an old couple. The child grown up and went on an adventure to defeat the evil.

The book is a translated version of that story. There are only two pantone colors, 3385 U and Rubine Red U, used in this book. The other colors are created by mixing these two colors in different proportion

Time: Spring 2015
Dimension: 5.5"x8.5"
Software: illustrator, inDesign
