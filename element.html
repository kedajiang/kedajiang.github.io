Element is a branding and packaging project of tea beverage. The concept behind element is providing naturally brewed tea without atificial sweeteners, flavors, or colors.

In terms of visual communication, the branding is achieved by utilizing four elements from nature –– rock, sunlight, sea, and plant. For example, rock is representing Oolong tea for it is usually grown on a mountain.

Time: Fall 2015
Dimension: 500ml/250ml container
Software: illustrator
